Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a new project?

We typically can begin new projects within 1-2 weeks of contract signing, depending on our current workload and project complexity. Rush projects may be accommodated with additional planning.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including maintenance, updates, hosting, and technical support. We believe in long-term partnerships with our clients.

What is your development process?

Our process includes discovery, planning, design, development, testing, and deployment phases. We use agile methodology with regular client check-ins and feedback sessions throughout the project.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We often collaborate with in-house teams, providing expertise in specific areas or augmenting development capacity. We adapt to your existing workflows and communication preferences.
